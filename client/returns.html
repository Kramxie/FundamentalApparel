Returns & Refunds Policy

Clear, fair, and easy-to-follow rules for returning products and requesting refunds.

Scope

This policy covers returns and refunds for orders placed with Fundamental Apparel. It applies differently depending on whether your order is a physical product or a service. Please read the sections below carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities.

Product Orders — Key Rules

  • Timeframe: Return requests must be submitted within 7 calendar days from the delivery date.
  • Required evidence: Before opening the package, you must record a short video (clear view of the sealed package and labels). This video is required for us to accept a return request. Upload the video when you submit your request.
  • Condition: Returned items must be in the same condition as delivered (unopened, unused, with original tags and packaging), unless otherwise agreed with admin.
  • Return shipping: Unless otherwise instructed, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We will provide an approved return address after admin review.
  • Inspection: All returns are inspected by admin. Refunds are only issued after the returned item passes inspection.

Service Orders — Key Rules

  • Refunds for services: Service refunds are handled differently due to partial fulfillment. Typically, approved refunds for service orders are limited to 50% of the paid amount after admin review.
  • Evidence & communication: Provide a clear explanation and any supporting evidence (screenshots, correspondence). Admin will evaluate the request and may request more details.
  • Exceptions: If the service was not delivered at all or was materially different from what was promised, admin may consider a higher refund, subject to review.

How to Request a Return or Refund

  1. Gather your evidence: For product orders record a short video of the sealed package (required). For both products and services, collect any photos or documents that support your claim.
  2. Contact us: Send your request through our Returns page or email support@fundamental.com. Include your Order ID, delivery date, email, the reason for return/refund, and attach the required evidence files.
  3. Admin review: Our admin team will review your submission and may ask follow-up questions. Expect a response within 3 business days.
  4. Return approval & instructions: If approved, admin will provide return instructions including the approved shipping address and next steps.
  5. Refund processing: Once we receive and inspect the returned item (for products) or complete the review (for services), the owner will process the refund manually via bank transfer. We will email you confirmation and transfer details.

Refund Timing & Transfer

After admin approval and any required inspection, refunds are processed by manual bank transfer. The owner initiates the transfer and we will send an email confirmation once the transfer is sent. Bank processing times vary by bank and country; allow an additional 3–7 business days for funds to appear in your account after the transfer is made.

Non-Returnable Items & Limitations

  • Customized or personalized items may not be eligible for returns unless the mistake is ours (defect, wrong product).
  • Items damaged by the customer after delivery are not eligible for a full refund.
  • We reserve the right to refuse or partially approve refund requests in cases of suspected abuse or fraud.

Privacy & Handling of Evidence

Any videos, photos, or documents you provide will be used solely for the purpose of reviewing your return/refund request and will be retained only as long as necessary for that purpose. We handle your data in accordance with our privacy policy.

Questions & Contact

If you have questions about this policy or need help filing a request, email support@fundamental.com or visit our Contact page.

ABOUT US

At Fundamental Apparel, we craft performance-driven sportswear with precision and pride. Our journey began in 2019 with a simple mission — provide teams and athletes with high-quality, customizable jerseys and apparel that reflect identity, passion, and teamwork.

We’re a small team focused on craftsmanship, fast turnaround times, and attention to detail. Based in Imus, Cavite, we have grown steadily while keeping the same values that shaped our start-up: pride in workmanship and customer-first service.

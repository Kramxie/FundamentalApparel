How to measure

  • Chest / Bust: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, under the arms.
  • Waist: Measure around the natural waistline, typically above the belly button.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of the hips.
  • Inseam: Measure from crotch to ankle along the inner leg.

Note: These ranges are typical approximations derived from common e‑commerce size tables (ASOS, Uniqlo, general industry standards). Individual product fit may differ — always check the product-specific size chart if available.

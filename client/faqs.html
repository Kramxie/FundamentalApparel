Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about ordering, shipping, payments, and returns.

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout. Create an account or log in to save your details and track orders.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit/debit cards via PayMongo. Occasionally we offer bank transfers or cash-on-delivery for select services — check the checkout page for available options.
How are shipping fees calculated?
Shipping fees depend on your province and chosen courier. We show an estimated fee at checkout and compute the authoritative fee on the server before confirming payment.
Do you ship internationally?
At the moment we ship within the Philippines only. If you need international shipping, contact support for a custom quote.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within a short window before production/fulfillment starts. Contact support immediately if you need changes.
What if my item is out of stock?
If an item becomes out of stock after you order, we'll notify you with options: waitlist, refund, or similar substitution. Low-stock alerts are sent to admins to restock fast.
How long before my custom order is ready?
Custom orders depend on complexity; typical turnaround is 7–14 business days. The product page will list expected lead times for custom items.
Do you offer returns or exchanges?
We accept returns or exchanges for manufacturing defects within a defined period. For sizing issues, we may offer exchanges depending on stock availability. See our Returns policy for details.
How can I track my order?
Once shipped, you'll receive tracking details via email and on your orders page in your account. You can also contact support with your order number.
Are prices inclusive of taxes?
Displayed prices do not include any applicable taxes unless stated. Taxes will be shown during checkout if they apply.
How do vouchers and discounts work?
Apply voucher codes at checkout. Some vouchers may exclude shipping or certain items; free-shipping vouchers will be honored at server-side calculation if eligible.

Still have questions? Contact our support team and we'll be happy to help.
